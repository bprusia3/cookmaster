Recipe Of The Day

Looking for a nice simple italian dish! well this nice classic recipe of spaghetti is the perfect thing to do the trick and hs a cook time of around 1 hour. this meal serves at least 7 people and is great to heat right back up for leftovers. Here is a step by step on how to make this classic italian dish.

ingrediants needed

  • 1 bag of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar of tamato sauce
  • garlic powder
  • pepper and salt
  • meatballs (optional)

Directions

  1. bring a pot of water to a boil then put noodles in water and cook for 15 minutes.
  2. then get another pot and start cooking tamato sauce until boil. add meatballs to this if wanted.
  3. once noodles are done strain all the water.
  4. Then put cooked sauce and meatballs into noodles.
  5. next begin mixing everything togther.
  6. put 1/4 cups of garlic powder into bowl and mix.
  7. add a few dabs of pepper and salt and mix.
  8. let sit half covered for 5 minutes then serve.
