Casoh's Franks Red Hot Ramen

if your tired of the same old boring beef ramen from walmart and want to spice things up a bit. The franks red hot beef ramen recipie might just be for you. This wackey recipie idea was made by a famous stramer named casoh.


ingrediants Needed

  • marunchin beef ramen
  • Franks red hot (2 cups)
  • flamin hot cheetos(optional)

Directions

  1. bring 3 cups of water to a boil
  2. boil ramen packet for 7 minutes
  3. strain out most of reminaing water but not all
  4. put beef packet in and stir then pour 2 cups of franks red hot
  5. mix well and enjoy

ingrediants Needed

Directions
