Ranch Mac and Cheese

looking for a nice simple recipie for mac and cheese but want a bit of diffrent flavor to it. well here is what your looking for, the perfect blend of creamey cheese and ranch comined into a delicious bowl of mac and cheese.

ingrediants Needed

  • Choice of ranch dressing
  • Mac and Cheese Shells
  • unsalted butter
  • milk

Directions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside

  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the butter in a saucepan over medium heat until it begins to foam, then whisk in flour until smooth and cook 2 to 3 minutes. Whisk in the milk and seasoning mix and cook, stirring, until thickened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Remove from heat, stir in the cheese until melted, then add the cooked pasta, mixing thoroughly. Serve hot.


Air Fryer Pulled Pork Crispy Wontons

Want to cook somthing in the air fryer thats not pizza rolls well this is for you. this cripsy pork wonton recipie will suit your needs no problom with this easy and delicious recipie.

ingrediants Needed

  • 10 oz. Jalapeño Pepper-Jelly Dip & Spread
  • ½ cup green onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons BBQ sauce
  • Avocado Oil or Olive Oil for frying
  • 2 cups pulled pork
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 36 wonton wrappers

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the pulled pork, green onion, cheddar cheese, BBQ sauce, and 1/2 cup Jalapeño Pepper-Jelly Dip & Spread and mix to combine. Taste and adjust flavors. If it’s too dry add more BBQ sauce or a tablespoon of apple cider vinegar.

  2. TO ASSEMBLE: Place one wonton wrapper in the center of your palm. Place 1 teaspoon of the filling in the center. Using your finger of the opposite hand, lightly brush the sides of the wrapper with water. Fold over one side to create a triangle and seal tightly. Fold the two opposite points together and pinch together to seal once more.

  3. Repeat with remaining filling, placing the wontons on a pan or directly in your air fryer.

  4. Bake the wontons in the air fryer at 360F for 8-12 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown and crispy

  5. Serve warm with remaining Jalapeño Pepper-Jelly Dip & Spread. Enjoy!
